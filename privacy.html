Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 February 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SofTopia Ltd., a company registered in the European Union ("the Company", "we", "us"), collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application ("the App").

Information Collection and Use

We respect your privacy. The App collects minimal data and only when necessary:

  • Personal information: such as an email address, only if you voluntarily provide it (for example, when contacting support).
  • Usage and advertising data: anonymous data may be collected for analytics and to display personalized or non-personalized ads in the free version of the App.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC. Google AdMob may collect and use information such as device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to show ads, in accordance with Google’s policies and your consent settings.

You can learn more about how Google uses data here:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Payments

Payments for paid versions or in-app purchases are processed securely by Google Play. The Company does not collect or store payment or credit card information.

Data Protection

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we collect. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page and will apply from the date of publication.

Contact
